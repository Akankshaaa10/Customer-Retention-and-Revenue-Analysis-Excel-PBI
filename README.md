# Customer Retention & Revenue Analysis (Excel & Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional customer data to understand revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, and marketing effectiveness.

The analysis focuses on identifying key business insights such as total revenue, order volume, customer retention patterns, and marketing return on investment. The results are presented through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer the following questions:

- What is the total revenue generated from transactions?
- What is the average order value?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How does revenue change over time?
- What percentage of customers return to make repeat purchases?
- Which marketing channels receive the most investment?
- Is marketing spend generating a positive return?

## Data Model

The project follows a **star schema structure**.

**Fact Table**
- Orders

**Dimension Tables**
- Customers
- Products

**Supporting Table**
- Marketing

Relationships:
- Customers → Orders
- Products → Orders

This structure enables efficient aggregation and analysis within Power BI.

**Key Performance Indicators**

**Visualizations**
- Revenue by Product Category
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Customer Purchase Distribution
- Marketing Spend by Channel

---

## Key Insights

- The **Home category generates the highest revenue**, indicating strong demand within this segment.
- Approximately **35% of customers make repeat purchases**, suggesting moderate customer retention.
- The **average order value is around 1.1K**, showing customers frequently purchase mid-to-high value products.
- Marketing ROI is **positive**, indicating marketing investments generate profitable returns.
- **Email campaigns receive the highest marketing investment** among channels.
